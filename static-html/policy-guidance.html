Policy and Guidance

Please be informed that the The Consolidated Appeals Process as used up until September 2013 has been discontinued in in line with the IASC Transformative Agenda. Henceforth appeals are organised by way of the Humanitarian Programme Cycle. Appeal-related documents will be uploaded here until September 2014 to ensure continuity.

Please refer to www.humanitarianresponse.info/programme-cycle/space for information about the HPC and all documents relating to the 2015 Strategic Response Planning cycle and onwards.
