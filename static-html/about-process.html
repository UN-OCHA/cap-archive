About the Process

Please be informed that the The Consolidated Appeals Process as used up until September 2013 has been discontinued in line with the IASC Transformative Agenda. Henceforth appeals are organised by way of the Humanitarian Programme Cycle. Appeal-related documents will be uploaded to this site until September 2014 to ensure continuity and they can be used for reference purposes.

Please refer to www.humanitarianresponse.info/programme-cycle/space  for information about the HPC and all documents relating to the 2015 Strategic Response Planning cycle and onwards.
